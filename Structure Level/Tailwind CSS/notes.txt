What is Tailwind CSS?
Tailwind CSS is the framework of utility classes that help us to build the website a bit faster.

Here we have utility classes,
    Pre-defined classes for all the work.

Grade : 50 , 100, 200, 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900, 950
Background
    bg-colorName-grade
Text 
    text-colorName-grade
    text-size(sm,lg,xl,2xl,3xl...9xl) / text-[size]
    font-bold
    italic
    underline

Box-Model 
    padding : p-number ,pt(top) , pb(bottom) , px(left-right)  , py(top-bottom) , ps(left) , pe(right)

    margin : m-number ,mt(top) , mb(bottom) , mx(left-right)  , my(top-bottom) , ms(left) , me(right)

Dimensions
    width : w-[size]
    height : h-[size]

Hover & Transition
    hover:property (Immediate)

    Transition : transition-atWhatProperty, duration-[seconds] , timingFunction(ease-in,ease-out,ease-in-out)

Borders & Outline
    border-width
    border-style
    border-color
    rounded-sm/lg/xl.. , rounded-[number]

    outline-number : width
    outline-colorName-grade
    outline-offest-number : gap b/w border & outline

Flex
    flex 
    flex-col/row/col-reverse/row-reverse
    flex-wrap/nowrap

    justify-center/evenly/between/around

    gap-number
