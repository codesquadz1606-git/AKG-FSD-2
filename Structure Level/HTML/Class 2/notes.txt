Table tag
    Table is a combination of row & column
    where row= tr 
    column = td/th

    cellspacing : Space b/w outer cell & inner cell

    cellpadding : Space b/w content & cell

    they are applicable on td / th
    row_merge : rowspan (vertical merge)
    column_merge : colspan (horizontal merge)
